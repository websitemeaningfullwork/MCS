**\# MCA Website Design Specification (v1.0)**  
\#\# Project Name  
**Meaningful Career Academy (MCA)**

\---

\# Brand Identity

MCA is NOT a coaching center.

MCA is NOT just a course-selling website.

MCA is a Premium Mentorship-Based Educational Platform.

The core product is \*\*Mentorship\*\*.

Courses, E-books, Live Classes, Mock Tests, Career Resources, Community, and Learning Resources support the mentorship experience.

\---

**\# Brand Vision**

Build Bangladesh's most trusted mentorship ecosystem where students don't just buy courses—they receive guidance, accountability, and career direction.

\---

**\# Brand Mission**

Help students build meaningful careers through personalized mentorship, structured learning, practical skills, and continuous support.

\---

**\# Brand Personality**

\- Premium  
\- Modern  
\- Minimal  
\- Trustworthy  
\- Human-Centered  
\- Educational  
\- Professional  
\- Friendly  
\- Clean  
\- Apple Inspired  
\- Future Ready

\---

**\# Website Positioning**

Imagine a mix of:

\- Apple  
\- Stripe  
\- Linear  
\- Notion  
\- Coursera  
\- Arc Browser

NOT a traditional Bangladeshi coaching website.

**\---**

**\# Main Goal**

When someone visits MCA, they should immediately feel:

\> "This is where someone will personally guide me toward my career."

NOT

\> "This website wants to sell me a course."

\---

**\# Main Outcome**

Users can:

\- Find the right mentor  
\- Join mentorship programs  
\- Buy premium courses  
\- Purchase E-books  
\- Attend live classes  
\- Ask mentors questions  
\- Receive career guidance  
\- Track learning progress  
\- Build a career roadmap  
\- Become part of a premium learning community

Everything happens inside one ecosystem.
